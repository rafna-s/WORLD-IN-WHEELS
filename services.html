Our Services

At World in Wheels, we provide accessible transportation services tailored to meet your needs. Below are some of the services we offer:


Ride Booking

Book a ride with us and choose from various types of vehicles, including cars, vans, and buses, to ensure a comfortable and accessible ride experience.


Accessible Transport

We ensure that our vehicles are fully equipped with the necessary features for wheelchair accessibility.


Live Ride Tracking

Track your ride in real-time with our live tracking feature, ensuring a seamless and transparent experience.


Payment Integration

Convenient payment options through UPI & Card, making it easy to pay for your ride.
